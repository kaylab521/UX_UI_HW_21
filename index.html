About Me

HI, I'M KAYLA

I’m a creative and self-driven designer. Before I received my UX / UI Design Certificate from the University of Denver, I spent a year working as a victim advocate. My work experience combined with my education has allowed me to develop a compassionate and human-centered approach to solving user problems.

I am passionate about crafting beautiful UIs that deliver enjoyable experiences. Some of my strengths include my creativity, strong research skills, adaptability, and empathy.

Outside of design, you can usually find me writing or listening to music.
